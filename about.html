About SIET

Institution Snapshot

Sreyas Institute of Engineering and Technology (SIET) is a private autonomous engineering college in Hyderabad, Telangana, established in 2011 and affiliated to JNTUH.

Recognition

SIET has been recognized by IIT Bombay for online Spoken Tutorials and has a 5-STAR rating for that initiative.

Affiliation
